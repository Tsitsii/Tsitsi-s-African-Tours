Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe

The magestic Victoria Falls is one of the most famous landmarks in the world, located in Zimbabwe.

Table Mountain, South Africa

Table Mountain is a mountainous fortification stretching thousands of miles across the coast of Western Cape, South Africa.


Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania

Mount Kilimanjaro is Africa's highest peak and the world's highest free-standing mountain, located in Tanzania. It rises to an elevation of 5,895 meters (19,340 feet) above sea level and is not part of a mountain range1234. Kilimanjaro is a major destination for mountaineers and trekkers from around the world due to its relatively easy ascent.



Zanzibar, Tanzania

Zanzibar[a] is a Tanzanian archipelago off the coast of East Africa. It is located in the Indian Ocean, and consists of many small islands and two large ones: Unguja (the main island, referred to informally as Zanzibar) and Pemba Island. The capital is Zanzibar City, located on the island of Unguja. Its historic centre, Stone Town, is a World Heritage Site.
